# Task Ledger

> **How to use:** A single prioritized list of what needs doing, who owns it, and what's blocking it. Pairs with `/task-ledger`. Update the **Next Action** and **Status** columns first — they're what keep work moving. Keep it short; a ledger nobody reads is worse than none. Archive done items to the bottom or a separate file.

- **Project:** [Project name]
- **Owner:** [Owner]
- **Last updated:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

---

## Status legend

- **Now** — actively being worked this session/week
- **Next** — queued, ready to start
- **Blocked** — cannot proceed until a blocker clears
- **Waiting** — depends on someone else / external event
- **Done** — complete (archive periodically)

## Priority legend

- **P0** — critical / drop everything
- **P1** — important, this week
- **P2** — should do, no fixed deadline
- **P3** — nice to have / someday
