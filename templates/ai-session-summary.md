# AI Session Summary (Restart Packet)

> **How to use:** This is a restart packet — everything needed to resume this work in a fresh session, a different tool, or a different model without losing the thread. Produced by `/wrapup`; consumed by `/project-memory`. Fill it at the end of a working session. The final block is a copy-paste prompt you can drop into any AI tool to pick up exactly where you left off.
